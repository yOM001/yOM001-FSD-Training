MY INDOMIE RECIPE

So...

Noodles are a fundamental diet in many homes and I myself love to have a good noodle and I am sure so do you.

I want you to have a taste of a tasty indomie noodle so I will be dropping my indomie recipe for you to try out

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of indomie
  • 1 chicken portions
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 satchet of seasoning

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken in a pot of water for 10 minutes and fry
  2. Add the indomie and seasoning to the pot and allow to boil for 5 minutes
  3. In a separate pot, fry the egg with water
  4. Stir fry the indomie in a pan
  5. Serve the indomie in a plate and top with boiled egg and chicken portion
